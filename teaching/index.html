Teaching

My teaching focuses on tourism and hospitality management, customer experience, event management, responsible tourism, and applied business planning. I aim to connect theoretical foundations with practical cases, class discussion, and industry-relevant applications.

Hong Kong Metropolitan University

TRM B313F / TRM 3013BEF Customer Experience Management in Tourism and Hospitality

Junior undergraduate students · Lecturer and tutor · In person

Introduces students to the management of customer experience in tourism and hospitality, with attention to relevant theories, experience design, and the creation of distinctive service offerings.

HPM B305F Event Management

Junior undergraduate students · Team teaching · In person

Covers event planning, implementation, and evaluation, as well as current trends and issues in event management.

HPM B307F Advanced Tourism Management

Junior undergraduate students · Team teaching · In person

Provides a holistic view of tourism management through theories, cases, and applied discussion of tourism systems and practices.

HPM 4008BEF Advanced Hotel Management

Junior undergraduate students · Team teaching · In person

Examines the hospitality industry through theories and case studies in hotel and hospitality management.

TRM 3012BEF Responsible Tourism and Community Development

Junior undergraduate students · Team teaching · In person

Explores responsible tourism and the development of destination communities, with a focus on the relationship between tourism, local stakeholders, and sustainable development.

BUS 8060BCF Applied Business Plan (應用商業計劃)

MBA students · Team teaching in Chinese · In person

Supports students in applying management theories to real-world business contexts through the preparation of an applied business plan and related study-tour learning activities.

Temple University

STHM 4401 Digital Portfolio Creation

Senior undergraduate students · Teaching under mentorship · Asynchronous online

Guides students in developing an online digital portfolio that presents their professional identity, experiences, and career preparation.

STHM 4322 Designing Tourism Experiences

Junior and senior undergraduate students · Independent teaching · In person

Introduces the design of tourism experiences, including tourism hardware such as attractions and tourism software such as programmes, special events, and experience-based activities.
